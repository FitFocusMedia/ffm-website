4️⃣ Going Live (Step by Step)

⏰ Timeline

  • 30 min before: Test stream privately
  • 15 min before: Open doors in portal
  • 5 min before: Start streaming in OBS
  • Event time: Toggle "Go Live" in portal

🚀 Go Live Checklist

Step 1: Start OBS Stream

Click "Start Streaming" in OBS. Wait for the green status indicator.

Step 2: Open Doors

In Portal, click the Door icon (🚪) on your event to allow viewers to enter. Door should turn green.

Step 3: Toggle "Go Live"

Click the Radio icon (📻) on your event. It turns red when live. This shows the live indicator to viewers.

Step 4: Verify

Open the event page in an incognito window to confirm viewers can see the stream.

🔍 Portal Icons Explained

🚪 Door (Open/Close): Controls if viewers can enter the stream
📻 Radio (Go Live): Shows "LIVE" indicator to viewers
⏹️ Stop (End Stream): Ends the event and archives it
📋 Copy: Copies the event link to share
👁️ Eye (View): Opens the public event page
5️⃣ During the Show

👀 What to Monitor

  • 📊
    OBS Stats: Watch for dropped frames (bottom bar). Should stay at 0%.
  • 🌐
    Internet: If connection drops, OBS will show red/yellow status. Be ready to switch to backup.
  • 💰
    Orders Tab: Check the Portal's Orders tab periodically to see purchases.

⚠️ If Stream Drops

  1. Don't panic - viewers will see a loading state
  2. Check OBS status indicator (bottom right)
  3. If internet issue: Switch to backup (phone hotspot)
  4. Click "Start Streaming" again in OBS
  5. Stream will auto-reconnect in MUX - no portal changes needed
6️⃣ Ending the Show

✅ Shutdown Checklist

Step 1: Stop OBS Streaming

Click "Stop Streaming" in OBS first.

Step 2: End Stream in Portal

Click the Stop icon (⏹️) on your event. This archives the event and finalizes the recording.

Step 3: Confirm Status

Event should now show "Ended" status in the portal.

📼 VOD (Video on Demand)

If VOD is enabled for this event, the recording will automatically be available after the stream ends. Viewers who purchased can re-watch using the same link.

7️⃣ Troubleshooting

❌ "Stream not connecting"

  • Check stream key is copied correctly (no extra spaces)
  • Verify server URL is exactly: rtmps://global-live.mux.com:443/app
  • Check firewall isn't blocking OBS
  • Try restarting OBS

⚠️ "Dropped frames" in OBS

  • Reduce bitrate (try 4000 Kbps)
  • Check internet upload speed
  • Close other apps using bandwidth
  • Switch to backup internet if available

🔇 "No audio"

  • Check audio mixer levels in OBS (not muted)
  • Verify correct audio input is selected in OBS
  • Test with headphones to confirm audio is reaching OBS

👤 "Viewer can't access stream"

  • Confirm they purchased (check Orders tab in portal)
  • Make sure "Doors" are open (door icon should be green)
  • Have them try the email they used to purchase
  • Check if geo-blocking is enabled and they're within range

🎥 "Black screen for viewers"

  • Verify OBS is actively streaming (green indicator)
  • Check "Go Live" is toggled on (radio icon red)
  • Wait 10-15 seconds for stream to propagate
  • Have viewer refresh their browser
